About TEAM-dagarna

Two days, 4000 students and 77 companies


TEAM is one of the largest and most visited career fairs in Sweden where ambitious students meet potential employers. The fair is a collaboration between the student association of Industrial Engineering and Management and the Business and Economics programme (ELIN). The aim is to provide students in engineering and business with opportunities to get in touch with leading companies from all over Sweden. A few of the opportunities we offer students include:

Interviews

TEAM offers students the possibility to meet business representatives from a range of companies during a private conversation about jobs and future possibilities. This is a great way to establish a connection with the company of your choice, but should by no means be considered a job interview. Applications for the individual meetings will open in advance, and the meetings themselves take place during the fair, the 24-25th of September. Keep an eye out for information regarding application deadlines and companies that offer interviews!
