Information in English

Two days, 4000 students and 80 companies

TEAM is one of the largest and most visited career fairs in Sweden and is arranged for the 34rd time this year. The fair is a collaboration between the student associations for the Industrial Engineering and Management programme (I-sektionen) and the Business and Economics programme (ELIN). The aim is to provide students in engineering and business with opportunities to get in touch with leading companies from all over Sweden.
A few of the opportunities we offer students include:

Interviews

TEAM offers students the possibility to meet business representatives from a wide range of companies during a calm, private conversation about jobs and future possibilities. This is a great way to establish a connection with the company of your choice, but should by no means be considered a job interview. Applications for so called “mock interviews” will open in advance, and the interviews themselves take place during the fair. Keep an eye out for information regarding application deadlines and companies that offer interviews!


Events

During the weeks leading up to the fair as well as during the fair itself, we will arrange events such as guest lectures, case solving competitions, workshops and more. Keep an eye out for information about events held in English!
